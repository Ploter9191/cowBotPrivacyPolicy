Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 20, 2024

We collect the following types of information:

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

4. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You may request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
